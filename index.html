I wanted to express what I felt through the Covid-19 situation by photos and gifs. The crisis of Covid-19 has caused economic difficulties, space constraints, and change of daily life. On the one hand, it is also an example of the bad side of a contradictory and selfish modern people.

The word that I thought of as a keyword to represent Covid-19 is "irrevocable". The perspective of everyday has changed. It is strange and confusing as a landscape viewed through a kaleidoscope. The end has no promise and much is different than before. We cannot go back. It gives me an image of being stone slowly. I made a video of these things.
